Toro's Protein Smoothie

Description

After a workout session, I rush home to make this smoothie for maximum recovery. It has a significant amount of protein for my macros goals, and its a very refreshing reward for going ham in the gym!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk (personal preference on fat content)
  • 1 scoop whey protein isolate powder
  • 1 cup fat-free greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients except the peanut butter into a blender.
  2. Blend all ingredients thoroughly.
  3. Add the peanut butter into the blended ingredients and blend it in.
  4. Pour into a glass, and enjoy!
